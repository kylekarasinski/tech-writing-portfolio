About

After graduating from Southgate Anderson High School, I explored several majors, starting with Architecture and Graphic Design before ultimately discovering Computer Science. While experimenting with development in Roblox Studio, I realized how much I enjoyed programming and creating systems that interact with one another seemlessly. That experience showed me that software development was the perfect fit for my creativity and problem-solving mindset, leading me to the University of Detroit Mercy’s five-year Computer Science to Software Engineering program. Lately, I’ve become especially interested in game development because it allows me to build experiences that others can interact with and enjoy.
